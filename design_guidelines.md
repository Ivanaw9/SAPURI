# SAPURI GROUP Website Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from industrial B2B websites like Alibaba, Thomasnet, and industry-specific equipment suppliers. Focus on professional credibility, product showcasing, and lead generation optimization.

## Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Brand Blue: 210 85% 35% (deep professional blue)
- Accent Orange: 25 90% 55% (warm industrial orange)
- Text Dark: 220 15% 20% (charcoal)
- Text Light: 0 0% 95% (off-white)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Background Light: 210 20% 98% (subtle blue-tinted white)
- Background Dark: 220 25% 8% (deep navy for dark mode)
- Border Light: 210 15% 85% (light gray-blue)
- Success Green: 145 65% 45% (for quotation confirmations)

## Typography
**Font Stack**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: Inter Bold (700) - H1: 48px, H2: 36px, H3: 24px
- Body: Inter Regular (400) - 16px base, 18px for product descriptions
- Captions: Inter Medium (500) - 14px for specifications, labels

## Layout System
**Spacing Units**: Tailwind spacing primitives of 2, 4, 6, and 8
- Micro spacing: p-2, m-2 (8px)
- Standard spacing: p-4, m-4 (16px) 
- Section spacing: p-6, m-6 (24px)
- Large spacing: p-8, m-8 (32px)

## Component Library

### Navigation
- Sticky header with mega-menu for product categories
- Breadcrumb navigation for product pages
- Mobile hamburger menu with slide-out drawer

### Product Display
- Grid layouts: 3-column desktop, 2-column tablet, 1-column mobile
- Product cards with hover effects and "Request Quote" CTAs
- Image galleries with zoom functionality
- Specification tables with alternating row backgrounds

### Forms & CTAs
- Primary buttons: Solid blue with orange hover state
- Secondary buttons: Outline style with blue border
- Quote request forms with floating labels
- WhatsApp integration button (fixed bottom-right)

### Content Sections
- Hero section with large machinery images and value proposition
- Featured products carousel with navigation dots
- Article preview cards with publication dates
- Company stats/achievements with animated counters

## Images
**Hero Image**: Large industrial machinery photograph (1920x800px) showing SAPURI equipment in action, with gradient overlay for text readability.

**Product Images**: High-resolution equipment photos with consistent white/light gray backgrounds. Multiple angles for each product.

**Section Backgrounds**: Subtle geometric patterns or light industrial textures for visual interest without distraction.

**Company Photos**: Professional shots of facilities, team members, and client installations for About Us section.

## Key Design Principles
1. **Professional Authority**: Clean, structured layouts that build trust
2. **Product-Centric**: Visual hierarchy prioritizing equipment specifications and capabilities
3. **Lead Generation**: Strategic placement of quotation forms and contact CTAs
4. **Mobile Optimization**: Touch-friendly interfaces for busy professionals
5. **Performance**: Fast loading for technical specifications and large product catalogs
